## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive SEO audit of **TITAN GYM**, a local fitness center located in Gujaini, Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh. The objective is to evaluate the business's online presence, identify SEO issues, and provide practical recommendations to improve search engine rankings and local visibility.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze the online presence of TITAN GYM.
- Evaluate Local SEO performance.
- Identify technical and on-page SEO issues.
- Suggest actionable improvements.
- Improve search engine visibility and customer engagement.

## Key Findings

### Strengths
- Active Google Business Profile
- Strong customer ratings
- Good local visibility
- Accurate business information

### Areas for Improvement
- No official website
- Limited technical SEO
- No optimized meta tags
- No content marketing strategy
- No Google Analytics integration

---

## Recommendations

- Develop an SEO-friendly website.
- Optimize title tags and meta descriptions.
- Use local keywords such as:
  - Gym in Gujaini
  - Best Gym in Kanpur
  - Fitness Center in Kanpur
- Create fitness-related blog posts.
- Add high-quality images with ALT text.
- Integrate Google Analytics and Google Search Console.
- Improve local citations and backlinks.
